# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Focus feedback on ideas and code, not on people
- Welcome contributors of all backgrounds and experience levels

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment
- Spam or off-topic content

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported by opening a private GitHub Security Advisory or contacting the maintainer directly. Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments or ban users who violate these standards.
